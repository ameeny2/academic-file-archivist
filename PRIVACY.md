# 🔒 Privacy Policy & Data Safety Promise

Your privacy and data security are the highest priorities for this
application. Because this tool handles file organization, sorting, and
cleanup, we believe in complete transparency about how your data is treated.

## 1. 100% Local Processing

- **Zero cloud uploads.** The application operates entirely on your
  physical machine. The AI model runs locally, offline.
- **No server connectivity.** Your files, filenames, folder structures,
  and data are never sent to an external server, database, or third party.
  The only time the app opens the internet is if *you* click the Donate
  button (opens PayPal in your browser).

## 2. Why the App Reads Your Folders

- **Scanning & sorting.** The app reads storage strictly to analyze the
  folders you select — nothing outside them.
- **Cleanup.** Duplicate files are never deleted directly. They are moved
  to the app's own **recoverable trash folder** (kept for 7 days), and
  every run can be fully reversed with the Undo button.
- **You approve everything.** Nothing moves or is removed until you review
  the plan and explicitly confirm — deletions additionally require a
  per-folder Keep/Delete choice.
- **Protected areas.** System folders, program installations, game
  libraries, and code projects are excluded by a built-in protection list.

## 3. No Analytics or Tracking

- **No user tracking.** The app does not track usage habits, scan
  histories, location, or personal identity.
- **No hidden background processes.** The app only runs and accesses your
  storage while you have it open.

## 🛡️ Verify It Yourself

Don't take our word for it: you can upload the downloaded release to
[VirusTotal](https://www.virustotal.com) — a free service that checks
files against 70+ antivirus engines — and you can watch the app with any
network monitor to confirm it makes no connections.
